Why This Is Illegal

The Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act

Federal law (15 U.S.C. §§ 2301-2312). The warranty follows the product, not the sales channel. In 2018, the FTC sent warning letters to ASUS, HTC, Microsoft, Nintendo, and Sony specifically stating that "authorized dealer" warranty restrictions likely violate this law. PNY is doing exactly what the FTC warned against.

Build Redux's Undisclosed Sourcing

Build Redux sold hardware through unauthorized channels at full retail price without disclosing that the manufacturer warranty might not apply. Under North Carolina's Unfair and Deceptive Trade Practices Act (N.C.G.S. § 75-1.1), this may allow treble (3x) damages.

PNY's Double Standard

PNY accepted the Micro Center RMA without "authorized dealer" objections. Same company, same warranty type, completely different treatment.

Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act

Federal law says the warranty follows the product. "Authorized dealer" restrictions are what the FTC warned manufacturers about. You can sue and recover attorney fees.

State Consumer Protection

Many states allow treble (3x) damages for unfair or deceptive trade practices. NC, CA, TX, and others have strong consumer protection statutes.
