Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

ScrappyLabs ("we", "us", "our") operates the Poo Bear mobile application. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What We Collect

Voice Recordings

Collected

When you use voice cloning, we process your audio recording to create a voice model. Recordings are uploaded to our servers for processing and stored only as long as needed to generate your cloned voice. You can delete your cloned voices at any time.

Account Information

Collected

If you create a ScrappyLabs account, we store your email address and authentication credentials (managed by Supabase). Account creation is optional — the app works without an account using your own API keys.

Usage Data

Collected

We track character counts and generation counts to manage your monthly usage allocation. This data is associated with your account or device identifier. We do not track which words or stories you generate.

Text Content

Collected

Text you submit for speech generation or story creation is sent to our servers (or your configured third-party provider) for processing. We do not permanently store the content of your generations. Text is processed in real-time and discarded.

What We Don't Collect

None of the following

Not Collected
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or camera data
  • Browsing or search history
  • Financial or payment information (payments handled by Apple)
  • Health or fitness data
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking data

Device Permissions

  • Bluetooth — Required to connect to and control compatible AI toys (e.g., Poe AI Story Bear). Used only for device communication.
  • Microphone — Used for voice input (speech-to-text) and voice cloning. Audio is only captured when you actively press the record button.
  • Local Network — Used when you configure a self-hosted TTS or LLM server on your local network. Not used with ScrappyLabs cloud services.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to use your own API keys (BYOK), your text and audio data is sent directly to the provider you configure:

  • OpenAI — openai.com/privacy
  • Anthropic — anthropic.com/privacy
  • ElevenLabs — elevenlabs.io/privacy

When using ScrappyLabs services, your data is processed on our own infrastructure and is not shared with third parties.

Data Storage & Security

  • Account data is stored in Supabase (hosted in the US).
  • API keys you enter are stored locally in your device's iOS Keychain (encrypted, never sent to our servers).
  • Audio files generated are stored locally on your device. Files pushed to your toy are stored on the toy's local storage.
  • All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

Children's Privacy

Poo Bear is rated 17+ and is intended for adult use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app includes a Grizzly Mode feature (mature content) that is PIN-protected and disabled by default.

Your Rights

  • Delete your account — Contact us to delete your ScrappyLabs account and all associated data.
  • Delete cloned voices — Remove any voice clones from your account at any time within the app.
  • Delete local data — Clear all locally stored audio from the Audio Library in the app.
  • Export your data — Contact us to request a copy of your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
