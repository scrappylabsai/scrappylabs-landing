// Capabilities

Everything your bookkeeper does

Connected directly to your QBO account through Intuit's secure API. Every action is confirmed before it touches your books.

Invoicing

Create, review, and send invoices. Track what's outstanding. Follow up on late payments. Matches your existing numbering and terms.

Expenses

Record expenses by just describing them. Auto-categorizes based on your chart of accounts. Learns your patterns over time.

Purchase Orders

Generate POs for vendors, preview as PDF, and email directly. Supports both item-based and account-based line items.

Financial Reports

P&L, balance sheet, accounts receivable, accounts payable — all in plain language, pulled live from your books.

Email Monitoring

Watches your inbox for incoming invoices, bills, and receipts. Extracts the details and proposes entries for your approval.

Document Learning

Feed it your old invoices and POs. It learns your numbering, formatting, customer names, and product catalog. Gets smarter with every interaction.
